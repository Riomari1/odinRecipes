Low Sodium Salsa

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, roughly chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • ¼ cup cilantro or parsley
  • 1 small jalapeño, diced
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans no-salt-added diced tomatoes, undrained
  • ¾ teaspoon salt *see notes
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice or lemon juice, apple cider vinegar or white vinegar

Steps

  1. In the bowl of a food processor, add the onion, garlic, cilantro, jalapeño, and tomatoes with their juices. Pulse until it reaches the consistency you prefer for your salsa.
  2. Transfer to a plastic or glass bowl, and stir in the salt and vinegar or lime juice.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, so the flavors can meld together.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  5. Enjoy with chips, over omelets, with fish or chicken - the options are endless!

Special Equipment Needed

  • Large stockpot with a heavy, tight-fitting lid
  • Fine-mesh strainer
